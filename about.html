Background

I'm a senior Interactive Design student at James Madison University, focused on UX/UI and product design. My work lives at the intersection of research, accessibility, and visual craft — I care about creating experiences that don't just look good, but actually work for the people using them.

Accessibility isn't an afterthought in my process — it's a starting point. I've designed for users with mobility disabilities, seniors, and everyday people navigating complex tasks, and that range has shaped how I think about every design decision.
