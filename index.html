A little about me

Research-informed. Visually intentional.

I'm a senior Interactive Design student at JMU with a focus on UX/UI and product design. I care deeply about designing for real people — especially those whose needs are often overlooked. Accessibility isn't a checklist to me; it's a starting point.

Outside of design, I'm drawn to type, art direction, and figuring out how the smallest design decisions shape how people feel about a product.
